How to Request Account Deletion

Currently, you can request account deletion by sending an email to our support team.

Email: simats.git@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - [Your Email/Username]

Please send this email from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

Note: Deleting your account will remove all your data, including herd records and milk production statistics. This action cannot be undone.
